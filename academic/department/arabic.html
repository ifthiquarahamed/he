Vision & Mission


Vision

The Department of Arabic envisions emerging as a center that promotes a wide range of knowledge in its various aspects to serve humanity and to promote its advancement.

Mission

To produce high quality intellectuals and scholars by integrating the qualities of Faith(Iman), Knowledge (Ilm) and Good Character(Ahlaq) to serve as agents of comprehensive and balanced progress as well as sustainable development in India and in the Muslim world.
